Eco-Tech Building Solutions

Sustainability, Innovation, Beauty

The marriage of sustainability and cutting-edge building technology has shaped the foundation of Diasen’s philosophy. Founded in Sassoferrato, Italy in 1985, Diasen is a leader in green building technology, offering lime and local cork based products including insulating solutions, acoustic and thermal insulation, waterproofing and decorative coatings. Diasen’s products have been used in projects from the restoration and preservation of significant historical buildings to modern homes and public spaces showcasing innovative and sustainable design.
